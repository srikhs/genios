Photography Contest

Photography Contest

Photography Contest

Main theme of this event is “Technology and Humanity”. We are looking for striking digital images on Technology and Humanity – and their important connection to each other. The photographs should be the original work of your own.

 Instructions:

1: Deadline for submitting entries is 12.00 p.m. Indian Standard Time (IST) on October 27, 2013.

2. All entries must be submitted though our mail ieeegenios2013photocontest@gmail.com

3. Each entry should carry the Author’s Name; his/her complete Postal Address, Telephone Number, e-mail ID (if any) and your IEEE Membership Number.

4. Save copies of your photographs as low-resolution ".jpg" image files no larger than 500K at 72 ppi -- the size will vary. Set image size to approximately 720 to 1000 pixels on the longest side.

5. All photos must accurately reflect the subject matter as it appeared in the viewfinder. Nothing should be added to an image, and aside from dust spots, nothing should be taken away. Normal processing of RAW image files and minor adjustments to color and contrast are acceptable, as is minimal cropping. HDR and focus stacking are permitted as long as all manipulation is disclosed upon entry.

JUDGING:

The photos will be posted in our FB page and Website. The photo with maximum likes and maximum votes from judge will be declared as the winner for GeniOS’13 Photography Contest.
