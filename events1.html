Essay Writing

Essay Writing

“One damned thing after another" is how Aldous Huxley described the essay: "a literary device for saying almost everything about almost anything."

In exact dictionary definition 'An essay is a piece of writing which is often written from an author's personal point of view'.
Do you think you can write an exemplary essay? Do you think you can easily ace it like a piece of cake, then here is your opportunity!! Grab it with both your hands.
The Title of the essay will be posted on the day of the event October 20, 2013 IST 00:00
RULES:
1. Open only for IEEE members.
2. Only one article per participant.
3. The essay should be within 250-500 words, approximately 4-5 pages.
4. Essay should be sent to essay@ieeegenios.org along with your name, email id, membership ID, phone number. (Information provided will be used for administrative purposes only).
5. Entries should be in doc format.
6. The entries submitted only on the day of the event October 20, 2013 will be accepted.
The winning essay will be published in our website.
Selection Process
The organisers will appoint a fair and impartial judging panel to select the winner. The decision of the panel is final. No discussion will be entered into.

Drop your queries at: essay@ieeegenios.org

Slogan Writing Contest

Slogan Writing Contest

A slogan is a memorable motto or phrase used in a political, commercial, religious, and other context as a repetitive expression of an idea or purpose. The word slogan is derived from ‘slogorne’ which is an anglicisation of the Scottish Gaelic sluagh-ghairm tanmay (meaning ‘Battle cry’).

Are you ready to provide us with a catchy "slogorne"?? Do you think you're a creative, innovative master of words?

Then brandish your pens!!!

 Rules:

 1. Entries should be sent to slogan@ieeegenios.org along with your name, email id, membership ID, phone number. (Information provided will be used for administrative purposes only). Slogan can be a one liner or max of two lines.

2. The theme for the slogan will be declared on the day of event.

3. Plagiarism will be considered foul.

4. Only IEEE members are allowed to participate.

5. Participants cannot submit more than 5 entries.

6. Entries will be accepted only on the day of event Oct 20th,2013 between IST 00:00 to 23:59

Selection Process

The organisers will appoint a fair and impartial judging panel to select the winner. The decision of the panel is final. No discussion will be entered into.

The winning slogan will be posted in our website on the next day October 21,2013!
