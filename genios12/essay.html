GENIOS '12 - ESSAY WRITING CONTEST

Deadline - 23:59 IST Oct 2, 2012
18:29 GMT Oct 2, 20122 October 2012

“One damned thing after another" is how Aldous Huxley described the essay: "a literary device for saying almost everything about almost anything."

In exact dictionary definition 'An essay is a piece of writing which is often written from an author's personal point of view'.
Do you think you can write an exemplary essay? Do you think you can easily ace it like a piece of cake, then here is your opportunity!! Grab it with both your hands.
The Title of the essay will be posted on the day of the event October 2, 2012 IST 00:00
RULES:
1. Open only for IEEE members.
2. Only one article per participant.
3. The essay should be within 250-500 words, approximately 4-5 pages.
4. Essay should be sent to essay@ieeegenios.org along with your name, email id, membership ID, phone number. (Information provided will be used for administrative purposes only).
5. Entries should be in doc format.
6. The entries submitted only on the day of the event October 2, 2012 will be accepted.
The winning essay will be published in our website.
Selection Process
The organisers will appoint a fair and impartial judging panel to select the winner. The decision of the panel is final. No discussion will be entered into.

Drop your queries at: essay@ieeegenios.org
