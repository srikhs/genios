About GeniOS '12

IEEE Madras Section Student Network celebrates the "IEEE DAY" globally with the Online Symposium codenamed "GeniOS". Our fulcrum is to provide an easily available platform for all members to participate and entertain you with fun in learning. Participate and win special prizes and geek stuffs.

  • Exclusively for IEEE members
  • Prior Registration required for all the events
  • Certificates to be provided for all the participants
  • All the active participants will be mailed with an e–certificate in pdf format
  • Goodies for the winner of each event.!So,Participate and win geek items!!!
  • A registrant can participate in any number of events
  • Credits will be allocated for each participant's preformance in the event
  • The one who earned the maximum number of credit at the end of the day will hold the prestigious title "GeniOS '12"
