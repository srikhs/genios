GENIOS '12 - CURIOUS CASE

20:00 to 23:00 IST
14:30 to 17:30 GMT2 October 2012

Are you very good at Guessing? The “Sherlock Holmes” of your locality?

 Then here comes an opportunity to prove your reasoning skills.!!

Instructions:

 1. The link for participation will be available in the website on the day of the event

 2. Prior registration is required

 3. The game has four rounds

 4. The Participant will be given a crime scene to investigate the mystery behind

 5. The level of difficulty will increase as the rounds progress

 6. Each round carries two credits and hence a total of eight credits

 7. In case of going for a hint, one credit will be reduced

 8. The participant who solves all the four cases the earliest, with the maximum amount of credits will be declared as the winner!!!

Drop yor queries at: curiouscase@ieeegenios.org
