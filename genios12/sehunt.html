GENIOS '12 - SEARCH HUNT ENGINE

16:00 to 19:00 IST
10:30 to 13:30 GMT2 October 2012

No friends around? Wondering how to beat the boredom blues?

Then, it's time for the Online Search Hunt! Come, challenge yourself to see how many things you can find!

HINT: The Internet is an amazing source, indeed!

All we need to begin the hunt is some creative minds and an occasion worth celebrating.

What better way than our own IEEE DAY 2012?
IEEE proudly presents the ONLINE SEARCH HUNT to celebrate IEEE DAY (2 nd October, 2012 )

. Never heard of a scavenger hunt? Well, here's how it goes.

-> You have a preset time to find as many things as you can on the given list.

-> The one with the most items from the list at the end of the given time WINS.

Of-course, there are some rules,

-> You can use the online search engines.

->You can play it only ONCE, not whenever you are bored!

-> Only IEEE members can take participate.

The game will be open on the day of the event.

Prior Registration Required.

Drop your queries at : searchenginehunt@ieeegenios.org
